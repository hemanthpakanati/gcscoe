About us

Gemini Consulting & Services (GCS, www.gemini-us.com) believes in innovation and transforming ideas into new products and services through leading edge technology expertise and business domain knowledge. The concept of Gemini was developed by best and the brightest in the field of IT consulting, healthcare, staffing & recruiting and HR consulting to provide myriad services under one roof to its clients.

Housed with professionals, who are graduates of top Engineering schools and Business schools and years of industry experience under their belt, Gemini strives to be an international player in the field of consulting business. Having a strong presence in the St. Louis area and cross industry network of clients, the company is among the major players providing IT consulting services in the Midwest region.
