ABOUT CHATBOT

What is a chatbot?

A chatbot (also known as a talkbot, chatterbot, Bot, chatterbox, Conversational Entity) is a computer program is called android.

How chatbot designs?

Such programs are often designed to convincingly simulate how a human would behave as a partner, thereby passing the Turing test

How chatbot works?

Chatbots are typically used in dialog systems for various practical purposes including customer service or information.

chatbot name?

The term "ChatterBot" was originally coined by Michael Mauldin in 1994 to describe these conversational programs.

FEATURES OF CHATBOT

In the real world, we interact using natural language. You don’t need to press buttons or voice strict commands to find out how your friend is doing. You just say “hey, what’s up?”
